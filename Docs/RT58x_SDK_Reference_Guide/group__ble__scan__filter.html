◆ SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_BASIC_FILTERED

#define SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_BASIC_FILTERED   0x01

The Link Layer shall process advertising and scan response PDUs only from devices in the Filter Accept List, and a directed advertising PDU shall be ignored.

◆ SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_BASIC_UNFILTERED

#define SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_BASIC_UNFILTERED   0x00

The Link Layer shall process all advertising and scan response PDUs (i.e., the Filter Accept List is not used), and a directed advertising PDU shall be ignored.

◆ SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_EXTENED_FILTERED

#define SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_EXTENED_FILTERED   0x03

The Link Layer shall process advertising and scan response PDUs only from devices in the Filter Accept List, and the TargetA field is identical to the scanner's device address, or the TargetA field is a resolvable private address, address resolution is enabled, and the address is resolved successfully.

◆ SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_EXTENED_UNFILTERED

#define SCAN_FILTER_POLICY_EXTENED_UNFILTERED   0x02

The Link Layer shall process all advertising and scan response PDUs (i.e., the Filter Accept List is not used), and the TargetA field is identical to the scanner's device address, or the TargetA field is a resolvable private address, address resolution is enabled, and the address is resolved successfully.
